How to Make Cereal

What is Cereal?

You may be asking yourself "What the hell is cereal?" No worries, its not as strange as it sounds. Cereal is a desert (typically eaten for breakfast) made from the lactate of mammals (typically cows) and a mysterious grain & sugar mixture found in boxes (convienently called "cereal boxes") at grocery stores.

What you will need:

  • mammal lactate
  • a cereal box (preferrably with cereal inside)
  • a bowl (a cup will work too!)

How its made:

  1. Take a deep breath (this is gonna be hard, but you can do this)
  2. Pour the mammal lactate in the bowl... Just Kidding! Pour the cereal in first! (youll quickly learn this order is crucial.)
  3. Now that the cereal is in the bowl, pour the milk... I mean lactate into the bowl with cereal.
  4. Lastly, you will want to grab a spoon (oh yeah, did I forget to mention you'll need a spoon?), stir the cereal pieces around the lactate a bit, mixing it all in, then take a spoonful and enjoy!

Conclusion

Congratulations! You've made your first bowl of cereal and can now enjoy desert for breakfast!
